# Forebrain

Major subparts:
- cerebrum
- diencephalon
- limbic system
- olfactory bulb

Subparts of cerebrum:
	- frontal lobe
	- parietal lobe
	- temporal lobe
	- occipital lobe

Subparts of diencephalon:
	- thalamus
	- hypothalamus
	- epithalamus
	- subthalamus

Subparts of limbic system:
	- hippocampus
	- amygdala
	- cingulate gyrus
	- mammillary bodies

Subparts of olfactory bulb:
	- glomeruli
	- mitral cells
