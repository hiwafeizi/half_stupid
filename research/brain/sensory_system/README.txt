# Sensory System

Major subparts:
- vision (visual system)
- hearing (auditory system)
- touch (somatosensory system)
- taste (gustatory system)
- smell (olfactory system)
- proprioception
- balance (vestibular system)

Subparts of vision:
	- retina
	- optic nerve
	- visual cortex

Subparts of hearing:
	- cochlea
	- auditory nerve
	- auditory cortex

Subparts of touch:
	- mechanoreceptors
	- thermoreceptors
	- nociceptors

Add more subsections as needed.
